Community Volunteer Code of Conduct

Join Us as a Community Volunteer and Help Us Build the Largest Community of Software & Tech Folks in India.

Responsibilities

As an active community volunteer, your responsibilities will depend on your role. However, everyone must (try) to follow some general guidelines.

  1. Ensure community members follow the Code of Conduct.
  2. Participate in community team meetings and provide feedback for improvement.
  3. Encourage community participation in events and projects.
  4. Engage with community updates across our socials.

Volunteer Code of Conduct

Below are guidelines expected to be followed by each community volunteer. Please also read the code of conduct.

Be Curious and Have a Growth Mindset

  1. Seek new perspectives to expand your views.
  2. View feedback as an opportunity to learn.
  3. Make decisions deliberately with clarity of purpose.
  4. Be open to candid feedback without getting defensive.

Use Your Judgment Efficiently

  1. Make decisions with the community’s best interest in mind.
  2. In ambiguity, make provisional, common-sense decisions, communicate, and seek information to disprove if necessary.

Don't Be a Jerk

  1. Don’t interrupt or ignore others' opinions.
  2. Don’t bully, humiliate, or oppress community members.
  3. Don’t use intimidation as a tactic.
  4. Don’t attack people personally while giving feedback.
  5. Treat people with respect regardless of disagreements.

Collaboration & Work Style

  1. In disputes, community admins make the final decision.
  2. Volunteers aren't expected to work 24×7, and appreciation is expressed for their efforts.
  3. Build understanding & trust through regular communication.
  4. Take ownership of suggested actions.
  5. Provide constructive feedback and avoid arguing over the internet.
  6. Volunteers can leave the team at any time with notification to the admin team.
  7. Take breaks as needed to prevent burnout.

Liability Clause:

As a community volunteer, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • Your volunteer activities are undertaken voluntarily and at your own risk.
  • The community is not liable for any injuries, damages, or losses incurred during the course of your volunteer work.
  • You are responsible for your actions and any consequences resulting from them.

If you disagree with any point, it's recommended not to join. For questions, email us at forgenest.community@gmail.com

By accepting and adhering to this Code of Conduct, including the liability clause, you contribute to a positive, safe, and collaborative community atmosphere. Thank you for your unwavering commitment to making our community thrive.
